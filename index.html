IT’S NOT
ABOUT THE
TECHNOLOGY

We have a love-hate relationship with technology.

We love the versatility and rapid implementation. We love the ability to catalyse ideas and reach everyone everywhere. We love how the return is incomparable to any other medium. And we love how we can measure everything to prove it.

But we hate how easily the tech can get in the way of success.

When designers and developers call the shots, the focus ends up on not on people, but the bells and whistles of technology. When technology comes first, projects become more time-consuming and complex.

And when projects become overly complicated, they end up as an expense rather than an investment.

“Our underlying focus is always on your audience. By understanding where they're headed and what makes them tick, we're able to sweep your consumers off their feet, drive them to action and build lasting relationships.”

IT'S AN ART BACKED BY SCIENCE

At The Buzz, we think of ourselves as both artists and scientists.

For us, art is more than paint on a canvas or pixels on a screen.

Art is about human connections - creating influence and moving people on an emotional level..

When we make a choice to consume, the decision isn't based on logic or calculations. We’re driven by emotion.

We specialise in crafting art that triggers those feelings, and it's done through building meaningful connections with us, the people.

Like any good artist our workflow is innovative and adaptable and we strive to rework each piece to perfection.

On the other side, our scientific approach feeds our addiction in measuring and testing everything we do, to gather the latest insights and eliminate the guesswork.

“It's the synergy between both art and science that allows us to deliver fresh and effective campaigns that deliver time and time again.”

YOU SET THE GOAL
WE GET THE RESULTS

When we say results. We mean business results.

Here's how we do it.

1. UNDERSTANDING AND INFLUENCING HUMAN BEHAVIOUR

For every marketing action there is a reaction. The science is in anticipating the reaction. We use the latest in neuroscience, experimental and behavioural economics as well as cognitive and social psychology to make accurate predictions on how your audience will react. 

  

2. DATA DRIVEN DECISION MAKING

What are people looking at? Where do they spend their time? What are they searching for? When faced with marketing decisions like these, we don't shoot in the dark with guesswork but use the plethora of data available in the digital space to make well educated and insightful decisions that make your marketing budget go a whole lot further. 

 

3. STRATEGIC PLANNING

Our expertise and experience, allows us to make highly targeted predictions for what will work and what wont. Based on your goals and our findings we chart the course and explain each step, breaking down your goals into simple math. Nothing is left to guess work. 

  

4. SMART PRODUCTION

We don't try to fool you with a "Ta-da!" reveal of our work. Our work is done on iterative basis and with some agile methodologies. We keep you informed and involved throughout each step of our process. 

  

5. USER CENTRIC DESIGN

We combine usability design (often referred to as interaction design, experience design, IA, usability, UI or UX) with the dark art of PET (Persuasion, Emotion & Trust). Allowing us to not only optimise the user experience through logic and testing but also influence them through more emotional, psychological triggers. 

  

6. ONGOING OPTIMISATION

Launching a project is just the beginning. It's still a hypothesis. And thoughtful advance understanding only goes part way. We have lots of experiential tools, whether it's observational, like on-screen eye tracking, or cultural, like looking at ethnography, or usability focused, like interaction design trends. To find the perfect answers, we measure, track and fine-tune every aspect; so we can amplify the positive and silence

"The end result? Hard proof that your campaign is running optimally, delivering you the best outcome.”

About the client

La Chinata is a family business with no less than four generations of history behind it.

La Chinata's understanding of the role of olive oil in modern society as much more than a raw material, has pushed them to create a product range covering gourmet, cosmetics and health goods.

What the client said

R. Hernandez  |  Director

Services employed

- Email marketing
- Conversion optimisation strategy
- Content strategy


Challenge

La Chinata were planning to produce a new line of olive oil products to the market, branching out from natural oils used for cooking to a much wider range including health, cosmetics and fragrances.

La Chinata approached The Buzz as they were aware of the limitations of their existing digital presence. It was clear the internet had not been an active part of their marketing strategy and could work much harder to promote their brand.

Solution

With a broad and active target audience. La Chinata lacked the means to communicate directly with their existing customer base and to attract new customers interested in their product range.

1. Our digital strategy involved creating a new, optimised website that would boost the brand presence whilst maximing the conversion rate of each visitor.

2. We created a permission marketing campaign allowing La Chinata to nurture their relationships with existing and prospective customers through email and physical mail.

3. Everything we created was designed in an engaging and assessable format elevating the La Chinata brand, encouraging trust, loyalty and engagement.

Results

In the time working with La Chinata was able to achieve: 
– A 400% Return on investment by month 4
- A 50% increase in web traffic 
– New Locations in Korea, Mexico and the UK.

MAZDA

About the client

The Mazda CX-5 marks the dawning of a new era for Mazda. It is the first of a new generation of vehicles, a compact crossover SUV that embodies a balance between the best of all worlds: fun, safety and sustainability, all in a very compelling package.

Services employed

- Content Strategy
- E-mail Marketing
- Interface Design
- Web Design & Development


Challenge

Mazda needed an online solution to promote the launch of their forthcoming family SUV.
Their main objectives were to raise awareness by showcasing the luxury features of the new CX5 with an end goal of educating the market and encouraging them to request a brochure or opt in for a test drive with their local dealer.

Solution

To fulfil these objectives, we advised the development of a microsite and once approved, carried out a full design and build service.

The design needed to be kept in line with the Mazda brand values and exude sports, luxury and style.

We created an HTML 5 interface which enabled the site to be viewed seamlessly on all mobile and tablet devices. High end photography and rich content paired with an interactive design and the ability to adjust car components, along with interaction in the social space were some of the many features included.

We ran three primary calls to action across the site:

- Request a Brochure
- Get specs & prices
- Request a test drive

Results

The number of test drives booked and brochures requested increased dramatically following the launch of the microsite:

- 140% increase in test drives booked
- 230% increase in brochures requested

The creative execution was engaging and on brand. The Buzz have been asked to work as a digital partner on future campaigns.
